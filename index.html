Biography

Eric Antonello began playing at the age of 16 following the teachings of various renowned Venetian teachers and immediately cultivating interest in metal, rock, jazz and pop.

Studied with Alex Stornello at the "Rock Fusion guitar" course at the Lizard Academy in Padua.

He began his live experience on national and international territory with many bands performing in hundreds of venues throughout Europe.

At the same time he consolidated his studio experience both as a session man and as a composer, giving birth to several works distributed all over the world.

He is guitarist for the band Arcana Opera and Burning Black and many other metal, jazz and pop projects.

At the same time he carries out teaching and session man activities
